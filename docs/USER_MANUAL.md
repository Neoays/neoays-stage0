# 📖 Neoays User Manual

## Welcome to Neoays! 🎉

Neoays is your all-in-one business platform to manage products, menus, deals, reviews, and connect with customers. This guide will help you get started!

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Creating Your Account
1. Visit the Neoays website
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Get Started"**
3. Choose to sign up with:
   - **Google Account** (recommended - fastest!)
   - **Email & Password**
4. Complete your profile with your business name and photo

### Logging In
1. Click **"Login"**
2. Enter your email/password OR click "Sign in with Google"
3. You'll be taken to your Dashboard

---

## 🏠 Dashboard (Home)

The Dashboard is your command center! Here you'll see:
- **Quick Stats** - Views, connections, and activity
- **Module Shortcuts** - Quick access to all features
- **Recent Activity** - Latest updates from your business

### Navigation
Use the **menu icons** at the bottom or the **launcher** to access different modules.

---

## 📦 nShop (Products)

Manage your product catalog and share with customers.

### Adding a Product
1. Go to **nShop** from the launcher
2. Click **"+ Add Product"**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name** - Product title
   - **Price** - Cost in your currency
   - **Description** - What it is
   - **Image** - Upload a photo (will auto-compress)
4. Click **"Save"**

### Sharing Products
1. Click the **Share** button next to "Add Product"
2. Choose your products to share
3. Select a **layout style**:
   - Modern, Classic, Bold, Hero, Promo, or Combo
4. Click **"Download"** to save the image
5. Share on **WhatsApp** or **Instagram**

---

## 🍽️ nMenu (Restaurant Menu)

Perfect for restaurants, cafes, and food businesses.

### Adding Menu Items
1. Go to **nMenu**
2. Click **"+ Add Item"**
3. Enter:
   - Item name & price
   - Description
   - Category (Appetizers, Mains, etc.)
   - Photo
4. Mark special items as **"Popular"** or **"Spicy"**

### Creating Menu Stories
1. Click **"Share"** on any item
2. Choose a beautiful layout
3. Download as an image for social media
4. QR code is automatically included!

---

## 🎁 nDeal (Vouchers & Offers)

Create digital coupons and special deals.

### Creating a Deal
1. Go to **nDeal**
2. Click **"Create New Claim"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** - "Summer Sale!" or "50% OFF"
   - **Value** - The discount amount
   - **Expiry Date** - When it ends
   - **Usage Type**:
     - One-Time: Customer can use once
     - Unlimited: No limit
     - Fixed: Set a specific number (e.g., 100 uses)
4. Toggle **"Publicly Listed"** to show on global feed
5. Click **"Publish Offer"**

### Sharing Deals
1. Click on any deal to expand it
2. Click **"Share"** button
3. Download or share directly to WhatsApp

Collect and display customer feedback.

- Reviews appear on your public profile
- Good reviews boost your visibility!

### Responding to Reviews
1. Go to **nReview**
2. View all customer feedback
3. Thank positive reviewers

## 💼 nBusiness (Business Profile)

Manage multiple business pages from one account.

### Creating a Business Page
1. Go to **nBusiness**
2. Click **"+ Add Business"**
3. Enter business details:
   - Business name
   - Category (Restaurant, Retail, Services, etc.)
   - Address & contact info
   - Logo and cover photo

### Switching Between Businesses
- Click on any business card to manage it
- Each business has its own products, menu, and deals

## 🆘 Need Help?

### Common Questions

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to Settings → Account → Change Password

**Q: Can I delete a product?**
A: Yes! Click the trash icon next to any product

**Q: How do I make a deal public?**
A: When creating a deal, toggle "Publicly Listed" ON

**Q: Why isn't my image uploading?**
A: Check your internet connection. Images auto-compress for faster loading.

### Contact Support
If you need more help, contact us through the app or email support.
